1. Pythagoras Calculator

Hypotenuse c
c = √(a² + b²)

Biomech use: resultant velocity, displacement, or force from horizontal and vertical components.

2. Momentum

Momentum
p = m × v

More mass or velocity means more momentum to stop, redirect, or control.

3. Weight

Weight force
W = m × g

Weight is a force, measured in newtons. Mass is measured in kilograms.

4. Speed + Acceleration

Speed / acceleration
speed = d/t · a = Δv/t

5. Force + Impulse

Force / impulse
F = m × a · J = F × t

6. Torque

Torque
τ = F × r × sinθ

7. Work + Power

Work / power
Work = F × d · Power = Work/t

8. Pressure

Pressure
P = F / A

Smaller contact area creates higher pressure, like studs or spikes.

9. Lever Advantage

Mechanical advantage
MA = effort arm / resistance arm
