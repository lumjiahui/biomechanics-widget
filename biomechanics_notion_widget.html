Biomechanics Cheat Sheet

StabilityLower center of mass, wider base of support, and line of gravity inside the base improve balance.
Mechanical advantageHigher when the effort arm is longer relative to the resistance arm.
Force absorptionLonger braking time usually lowers peak impact force during landing or deceleration.
Angular momentumChanges when torque is applied; conserved when external torque is minimal.
